Charminar

Charminar is a historic monument located in Hyderabad, India. It was built in 1591 and is famous for its four grand arches. The monument is surrounded by bustling markets and beautiful architecture.

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a majestic fort in Delhi built during the Mughal era by Emperor Shah Jahan. It is made of red sandstone and features impressive gates and walls.

Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is an iconic arch monument located in Mumbai. It was built in 1924 to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary.
